About Me

If you're the girl who plasters a smile on her face while in a deep existential panic, or the girl who keeps going after the worst has already happened because you have to get to the finish line one way or another, only to immediately crash out the moment you're alone: Welcome. You're in the right place.

The stories I write are for us. Stories about women that time forgot and history erased, yet whose fortitude and strength were the very vehicles that carried us into existence. Stories about female friendship and it's glittering, sometimes blindingly uneven textures, and about women who are left standing in the ruins of the lives they had planned for themselves yet still peer curiously around every corner, searching for their people.

Currently I am at work on my historical novel, Winds of Highwood. Based on a true story, passed down several generations, this is the story of three women on the Montana frontier in the 1880s, holding each other together while the men around them break, disappear, or betray them.

Off the page: My husband and I live in Southern California and have been happily married for 18 years. We are child-free, but are currently outnumbered by our pets 2:1. On the weekends, we can be found enjoying a delicious meal at Lingua Franca in Frogtown, or catching a AV ALTA game in our hometown.

— H.
